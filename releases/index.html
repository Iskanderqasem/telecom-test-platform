📋 Installation Steps

1
Enable Unknown Sources
Settings → Apps → Special app access → Install unknown apps → allow your browser
2
Download & Install
Tap the download button above or scan the QR code. Open the downloaded APK and tap Install.
3
Grant All Permissions
Open TelecomTestAgent → tap Grant Permissions. Allow: Phone, SMS, Contacts, Notifications.
4
Enable Auto-Answer on B-Party Phone
On the phone that receives calls → toggle Auto-Answer ON
5
Connect to laptop via USB
Enable USB Debugging in Developer Options. The agent will be reachable automatically via ADB tunnel.

🔌 ADB Setup (on your laptop)

1
Confirm phones visible: adb devices
2
Forward ports to Phone A: adb -s SERIAL_A forward tcp:8765 tcp:8765
3
Forward ports to Phone B: adb -s SERIAL_B forward tcp:8766 tcp:8765
4
Test connection: curl http://localhost:8765/health
